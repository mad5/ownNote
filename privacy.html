Privacy Policy for ownNote

This privacy policy describes how the app "ownNote" (hereinafter referred to as the "App") collects and uses information. The App has been designed to protect the privacy of its users and does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information.

1. Collected Information

The App "ownNote" does not collect any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

- Name, Address, Email address, Phone number, Location data, User IDs

The App also does not collect any other types of information that could be used to identify a specific individual.

2. Use of Information

Since no personally identifiable information is collected, there is no information used by the App "ownNote."

3. Data Transmission and Storage

The App "ownNote" does not transmit information to third parties or external servers. All notes and data created within the app are stored exclusively on the user's device and are not transferred to other locations or servers.

4. Contact

Since no personally identifiable information is collected, there is no need for user contact information, such as an email address.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

The privacy policy for the App "ownNote" may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updated privacy policies will be published within the app and on https://www.mad5.de/ownnote/privacy.html

6. Questions and Comments

If you have any questions or comments about the privacy policy or the use of the App "ownNote," you can contact us: ownNote@flipflox.de.
